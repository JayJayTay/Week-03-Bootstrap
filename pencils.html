Pencils are a great beginning tool for any and all artist. Pencils play a vital role in the artist process. It tends to be the first tool used to lay down the beginning of an art piece, this being the sketch of an art piece.


There are many different pencils that can be used to create different looks for a variety of art pieces. Some common pencils include:

Standard #2 Pencil Great for sketching and laying foundations of an overall art piece
Colored Pencil Great for adding pops of color to your art
Charcoal Pencil Great for getting darker shades and creating really great contrast
